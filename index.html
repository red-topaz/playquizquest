Why Quiz Quest?

Higher-level classes demand more than flashcards and generic quizzes.

The problem

As students progress into higher-level classes, especially in STEM fields, they often struggle to find effective study tools tailored to their needs. Existing software focuses heavily on mastering one subject at a rudimentary level, using rigid study frameworks that don’t leave room for the intensive, in-depth work required in STEM majors.

Our solution

Quiz Quest is revolutionizing this space by creating a game-based platform where students can upload their study material, and the game dynamically customizes itself to their content. This approach not only makes studying more engaging, but also ensures versatility across diverse subjects and learning styles.

Our vision & how it works

AI + gaming + education, designed for university-level rigor.

1

Upload your materials

Students upload lecture slides, PDFs, readings, and problem sets. Quiz Quest analyzes the content to identify key concepts, problem types, and relationships.

2

AI builds the questline

Our AI engine transforms your material into quests, boss battles, and mini-challenges that emphasize deep understanding — not just memorization.

3

Play, practice, and master

Students progress through an interactive game that adapts to their performance and study style, reinforcing difficult topics and integrating spaced repetition.

Built for universities, not just individual students

Our vision is to empower students to learn smarter, not harder, by combining artificial intelligence, gaming, and education. By transforming study sessions into interactive experiences, we aim to redefine how students approach learning in higher education.

We plan to work with universities worldwide to integrate Quiz Quest into their curriculum. With institutional backing and verification, we can reach a larger community of students who need flexible, powerful, and engaging tools for advanced coursework.
